General Register Office

Official information on births, adoptions, marriages, civil partnerships, and deaths in England and Wales

The General Register Office is part of His Majesty's Passport Office and oversees civil registration in England and Wales. We maintain the national archive of all births, marriages, and deaths dating back to 1837.

  • Order certificates online from the General Register Office. It’s quick and easy to order birth, marriage, civil partnership, death, adoption, and commemorative certificates online via the government’s official website.

    Certificates cost from £12.50 including postage. In a hurry? Use the priority service from £38.50.
  • Apply online for a UK Passport.

    You can apply for, renew, replace, or update your passport and pay for it online.
  • Visit GOV.UK for information on birth, marriage, civil partnership, death, adoption, and stillbirth records and registrations.

    For corporate information, visit the Inside Government area of GOV.UK.
